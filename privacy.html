SEBIV takes your privacy seriously. This policy explains what information we collect, how we use it, and your rights. By using this site you agree to this policy.

Who We Are

SEBIV is an independent brand operated by its founder. For any privacy-related questions, contact us at sebivmgmt@gmail.com.

What Information We Collect

We only collect information you voluntarily provide to us. This includes:

  • Purchase inquiries your name and email when you contact us about buying a product

We do not use cookies, tracking pixels, analytics tools, or any third-party trackers on this website.

How We Use Your Information

  • To process and fulfill your order

We do not sell, rent, or share your personal information with any third parties for marketing purposes.

Community Submissions

When you submit a photo to the SEBIV community section, you confirm you are 18 or older and that you own or have the right to submit the image. Your submission is sent directly to the SEBIV team via email and is not stored on this website.

If your submission is selected and featured, your photo and Instagram handle may be displayed publicly on SEBIV's website or social media. You may request removal at any time by emailing sebivmgmt@gmail.com.

Data Storage

This website is hosted on Netlify. Netlify may store basic server logs (IP addresses, page visits) as part of standard hosting operations. We do not have access to or control over these logs. For more information see netlify.com/privacy.

Purchase and submission communications are stored in our Gmail inbox (Google). We retain these only as long as necessary to fulfill orders or respond to inquiries.

Your Rights

You have the right to:

  • Request a copy of any personal data we hold about you
  • Request deletion of your personal data
  • Request removal of any featured content
  • Withdraw consent for use of your submitted content

To exercise any of these rights, email sebivmgmt@gmail.com. We will respond within 30 days.

Minors

This website and the community upload section are strictly for users 18 and older. We do not knowingly collect any information from anyone under 18. If you believe a minor has submitted information to us, contact us immediately at sebivmgmt@gmail.com.
