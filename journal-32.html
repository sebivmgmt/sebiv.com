When I was younger I thought I wanted life to be easy. That all my problems would go away by ignoring them. If I just didn't go to school then my teacher would never be able to ask about that homework I didn't turn in.

I have always been awful with procrastination. I wait until the very last minute. I used to wait until the pit was so big in my stomach that I would be forced into doing it.

2025 is the year I stopped procrastinating. I can't even tell you the moment it clicked but like I've said before I think it all started w me making my bed.

Making your bed every morning gives you a sense of responsibility for your day. Like you have control of it. For your morning routine will determine the mood of your entire day. Your weekdays don't have to revolve around work. Put yourself first.

Yesterday was extensive work on the score. I completely reworked that whole aspect of the app. I still have no idea what I'm doing but the more I do it the closer I feel to a billionaire.

The Builder in Exile.

I know I was meant for something. This could be the stepping stone.
