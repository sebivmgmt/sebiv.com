Son Of a Bitch Everything's Real

♪ Bonobo · Linked

"Everything you want is on the other side of fear."

David Goggins

Sobriety has been an eye opener.

Alcohol was my biggest weakness.

2023 is when I finally made the decision I would not drink until my name was on a deed — less than a year later I was hit by Hurricane Helene.

My apartment was flooded. I lost everything — but woke up with unopened wine bottles that had floated onto our lawn.

The restaurant down the street was filled with 4 feet of sand, and my 8 months of sobriety was down the drain.

After two months of nonsense, I decided enough was enough.

514 days sober now.

You have to make the decision.

I was always in denial that I had a problem.

The first step is noticing — but you have got to take that next step.

You will lose friends.

You will have to say no.

In a world where everyone wants to impress others — impress yourself.
