Frequently Asked Questions

What is SEBIV?

SEBIV is a limited drop concept. It's not mass production. It's moments, visuals, and energy turned into something you can wear or be part of.

Why is the shirt $777?

Because it's not just a shirt. It's the drop. It's the story. It's limited and intentional.

Will the shirt restock?

No. Once it's gone, it's gone.

How long does shipping take?

Orders are processed within 3–7 days. Shipping times vary depending on location.

Can I return my order?

Only unworn items in original condition are eligible for return. Limited drops may not be restocked or exchanged.

What is the upload section?

This is your chance to be featured by SEBIV. You can submit an image and your Instagram handle. If selected, your image may be posted or used as part of the brand.

Will I be notified if I'm selected?

Not always. If you see yourself posted, that's how you know.

Do I get paid if my image is used?

No. Submissions are voluntary and for exposure within SEBIV.
