004 About

SEBIV didn't start as a brand. It started as a feeling.

I've always been obsessed with music. Not just listening to it, but really feeling it. Especially when your vision lines up with the melody and you feel it deep in your bones.

I came up with SEBIV back in 2018 just gotten out of a relationship, wasn't in the best place mentally. Blue collar work during the week, raves every weekend. Music, visuals, energy… it all felt connected.

I didn't really know what I was doing. I just knew SEBIV meant something. Filed a trademark around audiovisual music, clothing, and digital content without a full plan. I just followed the feeling.

SEBIV isn't just clothing. It's not just music or visuals either. It's the connection between all of it.

Right now, everything feels surface level. The goal with SEBIV is to bring things back to something real. Real connection. Real energy.

You either have it or you don't.
