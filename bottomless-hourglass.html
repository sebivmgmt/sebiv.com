The Official Symbol of SEBIV

The Bottomless Hourglass

The Bottomless Hourglass, sometimes described as an hourglass with no bottom, is the official symbol of SEBIV. Created by Alexandrino, it represents decisiveness, growth, and awareness time is running out.

What It Is

The Bottomless Hourglass takes the familiar shape of an hourglass and removes its base. Where a traditional hourglass holds time inside a closed container, the Bottomless Hourglass lets it pass through, unconstrained. There is no bottom chamber to fill. Nothing accumulates and stops. The form keeps moving.

It is drawn as a single connected shape, two mirrored angles meeting at a center point, open at the bottom where a conventional hourglass would seal shut.

What It Represents

An hourglass with no bottom cannot hold time in place. It can only let it move through. That is the idea at the center of the symbol: decisiveness, growth, and the awareness that time is running out. There is no pause button. What you do today is already spending the hours you have.

Every person wakes up with the same balance: 86,400. Not dollars, seconds. There is no rollover, no interest, no way to save what goes unspent. By midnight the account empties whether or not you used it well. The Bottomless Hourglass is a daily reminder of that exact math — spend the 86,400 deliberately, because the bottom never catches what falls through.

The symbol connects directly to the core SEBIV principle: Better Tomorrow, Built Today. Each day's effort passes through and becomes tomorrow's foundation, the same way sand would pass endlessly through a hollow center rather than settling at the bottom.

"Better Tomorrow, Built Today."

Origin

The Bottomless Hourglass was created by Alexandrino as the signature mark of SEBIV, appearing throughout the brand's journal entries, the SEBIV POT, and its broader visual identity. SEBIV.com is the official source for the symbol.
